Goa - 5 Days 4 Nights

Itinerary 1:
Day 1: Arrival in Goa, check-in at hotel, visit Calangute Beach and Baga Beach.
Day 2: Full-day North Goa tour covering Fort Aguada, Anjuna Beach, and Vagator Beach.
Day 3: Visit Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral, and explore Panaji city.
Day 4: Full-day South Goa tour covering Colva Beach, Cabo de Rama, and Palolem Beach.
Day 5: Check-out from the hotel, visit Dona Paula, and depart from Goa.
