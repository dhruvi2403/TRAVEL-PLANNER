Manali - 5 Days 4 Nights

Itinerary 2:
Day 1: Arrival in Manali, check-in at hotel, relax and enjoy the scenic beauty.
Day 2: Visit Vashisht Temple and Hot Springs, then explore the Old Manali area.
Day 3: Excursion to Rohtang Pass and spend time in the snow.
Day 4: Explore Naggar Castle and Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery, followed by an evening stroll on Mall Road.
Day 5: Check-out from the hotel, visit Manu Temple, and depart from Manali.
