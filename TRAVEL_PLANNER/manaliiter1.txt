Manali - 5 Days 4 Nights

Itinerary 1:
Day 1: Arrival in Manali, check-in at hotel, visit Hadimba Temple, and explore Mall Road.
Day 2: Full-day excursion to Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding and skiing.
Day 3: Visit Rohtang Pass (subject to availability) and enjoy the snow-covered landscapes.
Day 4: Explore Manikaran Hot Springs and Kullu Valley, followed by a visit to the local markets.
Day 5: Check-out from the hotel, visit Tibetan Monastery, and depart from Manali.
