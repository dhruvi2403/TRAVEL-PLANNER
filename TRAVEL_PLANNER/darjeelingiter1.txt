Darjeeling - 6 Days 5 Nights

Itinerary 1:
Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling, check-in at hotel, and visit Darjeeling Mall (Chowrasta).
Day 2: Early morning visit to Tiger Hill for sunrise, followed by visits to Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop.
Day 3: Full-day excursion to Mirik, visit Sumendu Lake, and enjoy boating.
Day 4: Visit Peace Pagoda, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, and the Zoo.
Day 5: Visit Tenzing Rock, Tea Garden, and Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Center.
Day 6: Check-out from the hotel, visit the local market, and depart from Darjeeling.
