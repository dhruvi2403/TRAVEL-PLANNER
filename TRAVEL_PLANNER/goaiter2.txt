Goa - 5 Days 4 Nights

Itinerary 2:
Day 1: Arrival in Goa, check-in at hotel, relax at the beach.
Day 2: Visit Dudhsagar Waterfalls, and explore the spice plantations.
Day 3: Explore Old Goa, visit Basilica of Bom Jesus and St. Augustine Tower.
Day 4: Full-day tour to the beaches of South Goa including Colva, Benaulim, and Mobor Beach.
Day 5: Check-out from the hotel, visit the local markets, and depart from Goa
