Darjeeling - 6 Days 5 Nights

Itinerary 2:
Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling, check-in at hotel, and relax.
Day 2: Visit Tiger Hill, followed by visits to Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop.
Day 3: Explore the Rock Garden, and then visit the Japanese Temple and Peace Pagoda.
Day 4: Excursion to Kalimpong, visit Deolo Hill, and Pine View Nursery.
Day 5: Visit the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, and explore the Darjeeling Tea Gardens.
Day 6: Check-out from the hotel, visit Mahakal Temple, and depart from Darjeeling
